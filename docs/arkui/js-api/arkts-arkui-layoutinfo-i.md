**Since:** 9

**Deprecated since:** 10

**Substitutes:** [Layoutable](arkts-arkui-layoutable-i.md#layoutable)

**System capability:** SystemCapability.ArkUI.ArkUI.Full

## constraint


Constraint size of the child component.

**Type:** ConstraintSizeOptions

**Since:** 9

**Deprecated since:** 10
